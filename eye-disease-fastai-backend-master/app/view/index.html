 Eye Disease  Disease symptoms
 Macular degeneration  a disease that damages the eye’s macula, located in the center of the retina, causing a loss of sharp, central vision needed for tasks like reading and driving.
 Cataracts  a clouding of the eye’s usually clear lens and is the leading cause of blindness.
 Diabetic retinopathy  a disorder in which the rod and cone cells in the retina deteriorate causing impaired or loss of vision.
 Glaucoma  a disease that gradually damages the optic nerve that carries visual signals from the eye to the brain.
